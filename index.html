About US

Taxi and Limousine Services

Are you planning a trip to Bengaluru and in need of a reliable and comfortable transportation option? Look no further! DJ Travels, offers a wide range of vehicles to suit all your travel needs, including airport transfers, business travel, corporate travel, local and outstation journeys, and more. Whether you need a ride to or from the airport, transportation for your business meetings, or a comfortable vehicle for your corporate clients, we have got you covered. Our fleet of vehicles includes SUVs, sedans, luxury cars, buses and more. We ensure that all our vehicles are well-maintained and equipped with modern amenities to make your journey comfortable and enjoyable. Our experienced drivers are professional and knowledgeable about the city, ensuring that you reach your destination on time and without any hassle. We also offer customizable tour packages to help you explore the best of Bengaluru's attractions and sights. So, whether you are a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveller to Bengaluru, DJ Travels is here to make your trip a memorable one
